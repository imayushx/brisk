APP BUILDER

I build apps. Brisk is one of them. I got frustrated that every finance tool out there assumes you already speak the language — RSI, MACD, moving averages. They hand you a chart and expect you to figure it out.

Most people don't have time for that. They just want to know: is this worth looking at right now, or not?

So I built Brisk. It does all the analysis behind the scenes and gives you one clear answer — with the honest reason, and the catch. No jargon, no noise, no condescension.

It's early. It's independent. And it's built to actually be useful — not to impress other developers.
