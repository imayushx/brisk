// what the signals mean

THREE LIGHTS.
ZERO CONFUSION.

We took the complicated stuff and turned it into three simple verdicts. Here's what each one actually means for you.

GREEN LIGHT
This stock might be worth a look.

The price has dipped but the company is still solid. Like a great product going on sale — smart buyers show up when others aren't paying attention.

YELLOW LIGHT
Meh. Nothing exciting happening here.

Not cheap enough to be interesting, not overpriced enough to avoid. No strong reason to act — but keep it on your radar. Could shift any day.

RED LIGHT
Everyone's already in. Late to the party.

When a stock runs hot for too long, the easy gains are usually already gone. Sit back, let the hype cool down, and revisit it later.
