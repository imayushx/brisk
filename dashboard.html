ABOUT BRISK
Built for absolute beginners

What is Brisk?

Brisk is a stock analysis tool built for people who don't speak Wall Street. We take complex market data — RSI, P/E ratios, moving averages — and turn it into clear, color-coded signals anyone can understand.

Every stock gets a GREEN (looking good), YELLOW (hold on), or RED (be cautious) signal with a plain-English explanation of why.

Who Built This?

Brisk was built by a solo founder who was tired of financial jargon gatekeeping good information. No hedge fund experience. No MBA. Just someone who believes everyone deserves to understand what's happening with their money.

Our Promise

We will never: sell your data, push sponsored stocks, or pretend we have a crystal ball. Brisk is an information tool, not financial advice.
